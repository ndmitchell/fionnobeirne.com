London based artist, Fionn O'Beirne is a figurative, conceptual and political artist, drawing inspiration from contemporary culture and politics.

Fionn O'Beirne's work is described as: "a personal and sometimes overtly political reinterpretation and insight into contemporary culture", and as "exploring with skill, space, time, and many varieties of image-making and capture".

Born in the USA and educated in Ireland, France and the UK, Fionn O'Beirne's first degree was in Philosophy and Anthropology, followed by qualification as a Projectionist and as a Barrister. Since 2001, Fionn O'Beirne has focused on art and exhibited in London and Norfolk.

Fionn O'Beirne is represented in private collections in London, Europe and the US.

This website has a selection of work. Other work by Appointment in London or at the Norfolk studio.
